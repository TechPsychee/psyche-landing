🎓 Psychology of Games & Play

Udemy Course

In Development

Turns out you can analyze games the same way you analyze early childhood attachment patterns. Who knew? I'm putting together a course about this—object relations theory meets Fortnite. How games tap into the same emotional needs as therapy. How a well-designed boss fight has the same structure as working through trauma. How marketing teams have been reading psychoanalytic literature and we just... let them. It's fascinating. It's also kind of terrifying.

Game Psychology Psychoanalysis Player Behavior Marketing Psychology

🌍 LinguaSpace

3D Language Learning

In Development

Here's a thought I had: what if you could learn a language by just... being somewhere? Like actually being there, except you're not paying $3000 for a plane ticket. So I'm building this 3D environment—let's say you want to learn German. You're in Berlin. Virtual Berlin. You need coffee, so you walk into a café. You need to take the U-Bahn. You end up in a library, a sports center. Real contexts. Real conversations. Not a flashcard in sight. It's either brilliant or completely impractical. Probably both.
