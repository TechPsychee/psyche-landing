Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to OneQ ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your personal reflections private. This Privacy Policy outlines what limited data we collect to ensure the App works properly.

What We Collect

We do not collect any personal or identifying information. We only collect the minimum data necessary to maintain core functionality and improve performance:

  • Device Information: Basic technical data such as device type, iOS version, and screen size (because we need to know how to not break your phone).
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous data on which features are used (e.g., how often people actually open the app, no offense).
  • Crash Reports: When something goes boom, we get a vague whisper of it through Apple's crash logs.

Where Your Data Lives

  • Local Storage: Your journal entries and preferences stay on your device. No one (including us) can read them unless you airdrop them to your therapist.
  • Optional iCloud Backup: If you enable iCloud, your entries sync across devices using your Apple account. We can’t see or touch that data, and frankly we don’t want to.

No Ads, No Trackers

We do not use any third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics SDKs. We believe in curiosity without surveillance.

Children’s Privacy

OneQ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children, because we’re not monsters.

Changes

If we add any new features that involve data collection, we’ll update this policy and actually tell you — like honest adults. Stay alert, but not paranoid.
